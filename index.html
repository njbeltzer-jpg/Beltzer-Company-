Surface Care Guide

The Curing Period

Fresh paint may feel dry to the touch, but architectural coatings take up to 30 days to fully cure. Avoid heavy cleaning or placing items against new surfaces during this timeframe.

Cleaning Best Practices

Use a soft microfiber cloth and a mild soap-and-water solution. Avoid abrasive chemicals, sponges, or high-pressure sprayers which can burnish the finish and alter its sheen.

Humidity Control

Wood is a living substrate. Maintaining a consistent indoor humidity level (between 35-50%) prevents the wood from expanding and contracting, which protects the integrity of the finish.
