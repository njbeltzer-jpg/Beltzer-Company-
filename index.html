Surface Care Guide

The Curing Period

Architectural coatings take up to 30 days to fully cure. Avoid heavy cleaning during this timeframe.

Cleaning Best Practices

Use a soft microfiber cloth and a mild soap solution. Avoid abrasive chemicals or high pressure.

Humidity Control

Maintain consistent indoor humidity (35-50%) to prevent wood expansion and protect finish integrity.
