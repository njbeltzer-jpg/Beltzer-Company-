From Log to Luxury

At The Beltzer Company, we handle the entire lifecycle of the wood. Our sawmill division specializes in transforming local Nebraska timber into high-end, architectural-grade lumber for builders, furniture makers, and custom residential projects.

Custom Milling

Precise dimensional lumber and live-edge slabs milled to your exact specifications. We work with Walnut, Oak, Maple, and other local hardwoods.

Kiln Drying

Our controlled drying process ensures your lumber is stabilized and ready for interior use, preventing the warping and cracking common in air-dried wood.

Architectural Slabs

Providing unique, book-matched slabs and character pieces for mantels, countertops, and statement furniture pieces.
